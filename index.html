About Us

Pro Health and Nutrition shop is a family-based business located in the city centre of Penarth which is a beautiful seaside town 5 kilometres away from Cardiff.

We sell a wide range of health products including aromatherapy oils, vitamins, other health supplements, herbal teas, natural remedies and already have a very good local customer base for our products. Please share the Pro Health & Nutrition Facebook page with your friends and colleagues as well as your other family members to let them know we are here to provide all their health and nutritional requirements.

I took over this business around 2 years ago and have made a lot of changes to the shop and expanded the product range, quality and brands. Some of the other products include crystals, rocks and gems and salt rocks and lamps which are very popular with my customers. My aim is to meet all my customer needs both locally and UK wide in the long run.

- Rana Owner
